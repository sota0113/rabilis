About Us

Fun, friendly,
and always improving.

RABILIS was founded in 2023 and is based in the Koto area of Tokyo. We're a tight-knit badminton community that welcomes players of all levels.

No seniority, no formality. Everyone uses first names and looks out for each other — new faces are actively welcomed from day one.

We're laid-back, but we're serious about getting better. Members range from entry-level players to those with inter-high school tournament experience, and we compete in monthly tournaments together.

Features

What makes RABILIS special

🏸
Entry-level coaching support

Advanced members actively help entry-level players with fundamentals — something you won't find at most clubs. We have players with inter-high school experience on hand.

🤝
Welcoming atmosphere

No hierarchy, no formality. Everyone goes by first names, and we make sure no one sits on the sidelines. First-timers fit in naturally.

🏆
Competitive spirit

We enter tournaments roughly once a month. Casual but ambitious — the perfect balance if you want to have fun and push yourself.
